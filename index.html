Welcome Statement

Legallia Foundation is a registered youth-led community-based organization founded in April 2016. Over the years,the organization has been embarking on so many grass-root initiatives designed to protect and promote the youths, and reject any thing that contribute to the suffering of youths in sierra leone.It advocates, encourages and promotes all those practices that improve the welfare of the youths. And as such, the Foundation is centered on the empowerment of young people. Our key activities are; advocacy, training/mentoring and technical/financial support in any of the follwing focus areas.

ABOUT US

Legallia foundation is a registered community-based development agency committed of working towards a sierra leone free of poverty, hunger and environmental degradation. Founded in 2016./p>
